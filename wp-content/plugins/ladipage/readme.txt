Landing Page Builder for WordPress. Connector to access content from LadiPage service.

Landing Page Platform for Advertiser
Website: <a href="http://ladipage.com" target="_blank">http://ladipage.com</a>
Hashtag: #LadiPage #Landing Page

== Installation ==

1. Go to Plugins in your WordPress administration, click Add New, and search for “LadiPage WordPress Plugin”.
2. Now click at Install Now button.
3. Click on Activate after upload has completed.
4. Go to Settings and choose LadiPage.
5. Log into your LadiPage account.
6. When publishing your landing page from LadiPage, choose WordPress.
